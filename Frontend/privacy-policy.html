Privacy Policy

ExploreX by Swapnil Rathod explains what data we collect, why we use it, and how users can control their information while exploring places, routes, city services, memories, and local guides.

Last updated: May 18, 2026 Written in plain language so travellers can understand what happens with their data.

Overview

ExploreX helps you discover and plan travel experiences.

ExploreX is a travel discovery and route planning website for exploring places, city services, travel memories, and local guides. We collect only the information needed to run these features, improve the experience, and keep user accounts safe.

We do not collect your browser location unless you allow location permission.
Information

Data We May Collect

Depending on how you use ExploreX, we may collect or store:

Account DetailsName, email, mobile number, login/session data, and account role.
Profile ContentProfile photo, cover photo, username, bio, interests, and travel preferences.
Travel ActivitySaved places, saved routes, recent routes, viewed places, and travel memories.
Support MessagesSupport tickets, chat messages, ticket status, and admin replies.
Permission based

Location Data

We use your location only when you allow browser location permission. Location is used to show nearby places, route planning, and city-based services. We do not collect location without your permission.

You can block or reset location permission anytime from your browser site settings.
Purpose

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and secure your ExploreX account.
  • To show personalized places, saved routes, saved places, and recent activity.
  • To show route planning, nearby places, and city service recommendations.
  • To manage public memories through admin review and approval.
  • To respond to support questions, bug reports, and help requests.
  • To improve reliability, safety, and user experience.
Safety

Sharing, Admin Review, and Security

Private account data is intended to stay user-scoped. Public travel memories may be visible to other users only after approval. ExploreX admin may review public content, support tickets, places, services, and website sections to keep the platform safe and updated.

We use reasonable security practices, but no website can guarantee complete security. Please keep your login details private.

Control

Your Choices and Data Requests

  • You can update profile information from your profile page.
  • You can choose public or private privacy settings for travel memories.
  • You can request account or data deletion by contacting support@explorex.com, using support chat, or contacting the ExploreX admin.

Need privacy help?

Ask a question, request data deletion, or contact the ExploreX admin.
