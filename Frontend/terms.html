Important travel note

Travel Information May Be Estimated

ExploreX provides travel information, route estimates, distances, timings, and service details for guidance only. Actual travel conditions, prices, timings, availability, and routes may change. Users should verify important information before travelling.

Some ExploreX data may be estimated, including route distance, travel time, cost, popularity, ratings, and availability.
Platform management

Admin Rights and Content Control

ExploreX admin can manage places, city services, Kumbh Guide content, public memories, users, support tickets, and website sections to keep the platform safe and updated.

  • Admin may create, update, publish, unpublish, or delete places and services.
  • Admin may approve, reject, or remove public memories.
  • Admin may respond to support tickets and mark issues as resolved.
Limitations

Service Availability and Liability

ExploreX is built to help travellers plan better, but the platform may depend on backend APIs, maps, browser permissions, and third-party services. We cannot guarantee uninterrupted access or fully accurate third-party information.

Before travelling, verify route conditions, entry fees, opening hours, transport availability, weather, safety rules, and local guidelines.
