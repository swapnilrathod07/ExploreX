How it all started
From a Student's Notebook
to Your Travel Companion
"I was tired of Google Maps showing me the same 5 tourist spots. I wanted to find the real Nashik — the hidden waterfalls, the quiet ghats at 5 AM, the chai tapri nobody writes about."

My name is Swapnil Rathod, and I'm a Bachelor's student from Nashik. This project started as a college assignment — but quickly became something I genuinely cared about.

I noticed that most travel apps are built for tourists, not for curious people. They tell you where to go, but never why it matters. They show you photos, but never the feeling of standing there.

So I built ExploreX — a platform that treats travel as an experience, not just a checklist. With smart routes, rule-based trip planning, Kumbh guidance, saved places, and a community of real travellers sharing real memories.

What we offer
Everything you need
to travel smarter

We're not just a map app. ExploreX is a full travel companion — helping you plan, discover, and remember every journey.

🧭
Route Planner

Enter any two locations and get smart route suggestions with distance, travel time, estimated cost, and places to explore along the way. Supports Car, Bike, Bus, Train and Flight — with a live comparison table.

🛕
Kumbh Guide

A dedicated smart guide for Nashik Kumbh Mela — with crowd guidance, smart ghat routes, important dates, nearby facilities, verified emergency contacts, and simple suggestions based on time patterns and admin updates.

🧭
Smart Trip Planner

Choose a city, number of days, travel mood, date, traveller type, and starting point. ExploreX creates a day-wise plan with food suggestions, comfort tips, and route handoff.

📷
Travel Memories

Upload your photos and videos from journeys — keep them private or share them with the community. See traveller memories from specific locations and get inspired for your next trip.

🌐
Multi-Language

Available in English, Hindi, and Marathi — so every traveller in Maharashtra and beyond feels at home. Language preference is saved across all pages and auto-detected from your browser.

💬
Real Support, Not Dead Ends

Users can raise travel questions, route issues, bug reports, or help requests through ExploreX Live Chat.

🛠️
Admin-Controlled Content

Places, services, Kumbh guide items, memories, and homepage sections are handled from the admin panel.

📍
Built Around Real Locations

ExploreX focuses on coordinates, local areas, practical routes, and city-level services for smoother travel.

Purpose & Direction
Our Mission & Vision
🎯
Our Mission

To make travel smarter, easier, and more meaningful for everyone — especially for people who want to go beyond the obvious tourist spots. We want to turn every trip into a real story, not just content.

Less screen time. More real experiences. Less FOMO. More genuine discovery.

🔭
Our Vision

A future where every traveller has an intelligent companion — one that suggests a practical route, groups nearby places, shows you what other real people loved, and helps you plan at a comfortable pace.

A community-driven travel platform built on real experiences, not paid promotions.
